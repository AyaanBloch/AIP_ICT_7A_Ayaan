Top 5 Tourist Places in Uttarakhand

Ayaan Bloch 7C

1. Mussoorie – The quaint mountain getaway
Nestled among the misty hills near to the capital, its an ideal quick getaway for travellers to escape the urban chaos quickly. The scenic town aptly called the “queen of the hills” was the most favoured hill station of Late PM Indira Gandhi. Apart from just leisure, the town also offers some nearby sightseeing spots like the Kempty falls, Sir George Everest house, company gardens to name a few. Very well connected from Dehradun by a scenic mountain road, you can also expect a fair share of snow here in the months of January and early February. It is surely among the best Places to Visit in Uttarakhand.

2. Nainital- Just relax by the lake one of the most popular places to visit in Uttarakhand
One of the most popular destinations in Uttaranchal, Nainital is an ideal getaway for couples and families alike. This makes Nainital one of the Best Places to Visit in Uttarakhand Nestled in the heart of mountains besides a scenic lake, people from near and far throng this scenic town in search of some peace amidst the noise. The small town has lots to offer in terms of stay and experiences with lots of things to do for people of all ages.

3. Dehradun – the bustling capital
One of the most beautiful capital cities in India and places to Visit in Uttarakhand , Dehradun is on the foothills of Himalayas and is home to many famous national institutes and schools. Certainly, It has a rich tradition of being the nerve centre of knowledge and education. As a result It houses numerous institutes of national repute like the Indian military academy and National forest research institute which are also architecturally aesthetic. Also, there are lots of popular getaways in the vicinity of the city like Robber’s cave, Malsi deer park, Sahastradhara etc to name a few. Most importantly, the city is well connected to other popular tourist destinations inside the state by roadways.

4. Ranikhet – Meadows and Hills
A serene cantonment town in the Almora district, its is the perfect place to escape the summer heat. The town has a rich history of its own, as it was a contender for British India summer capital along with Shimla due to its comfortable temperate climate and unparalleled mountain views of the Kumaon Himalayas. The town was also briefly ruled by the Nepalese kings in the pre-British era. It is one of the upcoming Places to Visit in Uttarakhand.

5. Dhanaulti – Camping and adventure
Tucked in the Garhwal Himalayas, Dhanaulti is an upcoming popular winter hill station as it gets a mild cover of snow during the colder months. The place is good to visit at another time of the year also owning to its mild temperate climate. Also, a lot of adventure activities are available to choose from in the region like river crossing, rock climbing, the overnight camp stays, short nature treks etc. Also Recently, there are a lot of specialised nature and adventure camps in the region like Dhanaulti adventure park and apple orchard resort.
